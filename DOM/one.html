Daily News Headlines

Here are some of the top news headlines as of Thursday, August 21, 2025:

India

  • Bills to Oust Arrested PM/CMs: The Lok Sabha has cleared bills aimed at removing a Prime Minister or Chief Minister who has been arrested for a serious offense and detained for 30 consecutive days. The bills have been sent to a Joint Parliamentary Committee for review.
  • Delhi CM's Security Increased: Delhi Chief Minister Rekha Gupta's security has been upgraded to 'Z' category and taken over by the CRPF after she was attacked during a public meeting at her residence.
  • Consumer Watchdog Penalizes Rapido: The Central Consumer Protection Authority (CCPA) has fined ride-hailing company Rapido Rs 10 lakh for "misleading and unfair" advertising practices and has ordered them to issue refunds to users.
  • Online Gaming Bill Passed: The Lok Sabha has passed a bill to ban real money online gaming apps to combat addiction and financial fraud.
  • S. Jaishankar's Push for Russia Trade: India's External Affairs Minister S. Jaishankar has urged Russian firms to increase their engagement with India, highlighting the need to reduce trade barriers and improve connectivity despite rising trade figures.
  • Mumbai Rains: Schools and colleges in Mumbai have reopened after a period of incessant rain caused flooding and traffic disruptions in the city.
  • Psephologist Under Fire: An FIR has been filed against psephologist Sanjay Kumar of the Centre for the Study of Developing Societies (CSDS) over a controversial social media post related to the Maharashtra elections.
